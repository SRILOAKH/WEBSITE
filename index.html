About Us

Little Brains opened its doors in Vijayawada in 2015 with the sole purpose of providing holistic education to children. Grounded on global best practices and the principles of “Individual Focus” and “Compete with Self”, the school strives to develop every student’s individual talent, creativity and communication skills, which are essential for success in future. We also believe in inculcating traditional values and a strong sense of morals for a well-rounded personality that defines the global citizen of tomorrow.

Events

For students to gain awareness on the significance of special days, Little Brains conducts a lot of events like Science Day, Hindi Divas, Mathematics Day, Teachers’ Day, Children’s Day, Independence Day, Republic Day, Red Day etc., and gives equal importance to all religious festivals like Ganesh Chaturdhi, Christmas, Ramzan, Sankranthi, Diwali, Good Friday etc. Children and parents take part in all the events with enthusiasm and excitement. It is a pleasure to watch the parents participate with full of joy and happiness as they get to play a part in their kids’ activities like Ammaku Vandanam, Mom’s Kitchen, and Women’s Day.

Our Driving Force

Little Brains is the brainchild of Mr Phani Prasad Muktevi, an eminent Child Psychologist and a School Administrator, who has rich experience of over 15 years in the field of Education. His firm belief that ‘Education should be the best adventure in a child’s life’, this is the cornerstone of Little Brains’ philosophy and the reason why he is passionately committed to constant innovation. His extensive professional and administrative experience, encompassing counselling and teaching, has benefitted teachers, parents and students by shaping their thinking to the importance of a good education, giving their lives new meaning and direction. A dynamic, result-oriented leader and an eloquent speaker, his vast knowledge of the field has seen him being frequently invited to speak at various prominent conferences on Education or Training Children. His single-minded focus is on promoting the academic, emotional social development and growth of all students at Little Brains.
